Designing of an electric power distribution system for industrial plants, residential areas and agricultural regions has become a challenging task. Intense research is being done in the field of power distribution in order to manage power in an efficient manner. This project is one such attempt to design a electrical power distribution system which distributes and manages the power distribution in an efficient manner by providing scheduled power cuts depending on the calculated demand factor and the supply capacity of the power plant. Authorized members can access the website by providing suitable credentials and check the power consumption of each region in real time. The power consumption of each region is displayed on the dashboard.
